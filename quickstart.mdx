## Prerequisites

Before starting, ensure you have:

- A modern web browser (Chrome, Firefox, Safari, or Edge)
- An email address for account verification
- Optional: A team domain for custom branding

<Callout kind="info">

Pakusaru is free to start. Upgrade plans unlock advanced features like custom domains and team collaboration.

</Callout>

## Create Your Pakusaru Account

Follow these steps to register and log in.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Visit the Pakusaru homepage" icon="globe">
    Open your browser and navigate to [https://pakusaru.com](https://pakusaru.com).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Sign up" icon="user-plus">
    Click **Sign Up** in the top right. Enter your email, create a password, and complete the CAPTCHA.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Verify your email" icon="mail">
    Check your inbox for a verification email from Pakusaru and click the link to activate your account.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Log in" icon="log-in">
    Return to [https://pakusaru.com](https://pakusaru.com) and sign in with your credentials. You'll land on the dashboard.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Dashboard Walkthrough

The Pakusaru dashboard provides an overview of your spaces and quick actions. Use the sidebar to navigate between **Spaces**, **Templates**, **Settings**, and **Billing**.

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Spaces Overview" icon="layout">

    Key sections:
    
    | Section     | Description                          |
    |-------------|--------------------------------------|
    | Active      | Recently edited spaces               |
    | Archived    | Inactive spaces for reference        |
    | Shared      | Collaboratively edited spaces        |
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Quick Actions" icon="zap">
    Top bar includes:
    - Search across all spaces
    - AI Assistant for generating docs
    - Export options (PDF, Markdown)
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

## Create Your First Documentation Space

Set up your project documentation in minutes.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Start a new space" icon="plus">
    From the dashboard, click **New Space**. Enter a name like `My Project Docs`.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose a template" icon="file-text">
    Select **Blank** or **API Docs** template. Templates pre-populate with structure.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Customize settings" icon="settings">
    Set visibility: Public, Team-only, or Private. Add a custom slug like `myproject.pakusaru.com`.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Publish" icon="upload">
    Click **Publish**. Your space is live! Share the URL with your team.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout kind="tip">

Pro tip: Use the live preview to edit Markdown directly. Changes publish instantly.

</Callout>
