## Start a Project

<Steps>
  <Step title="Access the Dashboard">
    Log in at `https://app.pakusaru.com` and go to your dashboard.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Start New Project">
    Click the "Create Project" button. Enter your new project's name.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Customize Structure">
    Add sections and pages using the sidebar. Drag and drop to reorder.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Invite Collaborators">
    Add teammates by entering their email addresses.
  </Step>
</Steps>
