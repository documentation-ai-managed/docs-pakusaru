## Problem

- Dashboard does not load
- Error: "Access Denied" or blank page

## Solutions

<Steps>
  <Step title="Refresh and retry">
    Log out, then log in again at `https://app.pakusaru.com`.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Check permissions">
    Ensure you are listed as a member for the required space.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Clear browser cache">
    Remove cookies, refresh, and sign in again.
  </Step>
</Steps>
