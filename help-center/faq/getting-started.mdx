title: How do I create my first documentation space?
description: Instructions for starting a Pakusaru documentation space.
---

## Steps

1. Sign in at `https://app.pakusaru.com`
2. Click "New Space" on your dashboard
3. Enter a project name
4. (Optional) Choose a template
5. Click "Create"

Your space is ready! You can now organize and invite collaborators to your Pakusaru documentation space.
